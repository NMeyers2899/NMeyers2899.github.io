Projects

Skyline Shredder

Skyline Shredder is a comic book-style third-person platformer that focuses on performing complex movements to navigate around an urban environment where your main goal is to achieve as much momentum as possible. Performing various parkour moves will rapidly increase the rate you gain momentum but failing to chain actions or stopping will rapidly decrease your momentum.

Dead Man Jack

Welcome to the brutal Underworld! Monsters roam free here. Led by their unrelenting master, Big Frank the King of Monsters!
It is here that Dead Man Jack has come to claim the throne from Big Frank. Separating his heart from his own body to give himself immortality, he plans to weave hordes of Big Frank's undead minions to claim the title. With his unique fighting skill, and the freshly opened hole in his chest, he's able to use the abilities of the enemies he grabs and swallows against their own allies.
Fight like your unlife depends on it!
